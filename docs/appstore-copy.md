## Description

Your period data is some of the most sensitive information on your phone. After Flo Health's $59.5M settlement for sharing period data with Facebook and Google — Caelyn was built differently from day one.

**No server. No account. No compromise.**

Everything you log — your cycles, symptoms, moods, notes — lives only on your iPhone. We have no database. We cannot see your data even if we wanted to. There is nothing to hand over.
