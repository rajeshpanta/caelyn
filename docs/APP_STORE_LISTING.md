## Full Description (up to 4,000 characters)

### Caelyn — Your Cycle, Your Body, Your Phone

#### Learn Your Body. Can't Tell Anyone.

Every cycle app promises to know you better. Caelyn actually does—and is structurally incapable of selling, sharing, or losing that knowledge.

Your cycle data never leaves your phone. No server. No account. No third-party trackers. Caelyn runs entirely on device, learning your personal luteal length, your true PMS onset, and your fertile window from the logs only you can see. The same intelligence that makes competitors sticky—except verifiable and warrant-proof because there's nowhere else for your data to go.

#### What Caelyn Does

**Understand Your Cycle**
- See your exact cycle day, phase (menstrual, follicular, ovulation, luteal, PMS), and how far through each you are
- Get predictions of your next period, PMS window, and fertile days—accurate by week 2, precise by cycle 3
- Discover which symptoms cluster together and when they typically arrive in your cycle
- Track irregular cycles without losing predictions, or switch to period-specific modes (trying to conceive, pregnant, postpartum)

**Log What Matters**
- Flow, pain (location and severity), mood, energy, basal body temperature, ovulation tests
- 11 core symptoms plus condition-specific sets (PCOS, endometriosis, perimenopause); add your own
- Notes, medication, sexual activity, cervical mucus, pregnancy tests—log what's relevant to you
- Export everything as CSV anytime; use PDF reports for doctor visits

**Stay One Step Ahead**
- Coming Up timeline shows your next period, fertile window, and predicted PMS—up to 3 months out
- Pattern cards surface early signals: which symptom clusters before your period, typical pain trend, predicted mood dips
- TTC (trying to conceive) fertility dashboard with daily scoring and signal summaries (Pro)
- Lock-screen widgets for at-a-glance phase and period countdown (Pro)
- Apple Watch app and mini-widgets for discreet checking and logging

**Get Intelligent Insights**
- After 3 cycles, Caelyn shows "what we learned about you": your real PMS timing, your luteal length, your pain trend
- Charts and distributions for cycle length, period flow, symptom patterns, mood, and temperature (Pro)
- Year-in-review calendar grid and cycle comparisons (Pro)
- All patterns generated on-device; you're never a data point in an algorithm

#### Privacy by Architecture

No marketing promises. No fine print. Just honest building blocks:

**On-Device Only**
- Every entry, every learned pattern, every prediction happens on your phone using Apple's Foundation Models (with a rule-based fallback)
- App Lock with Face ID, Touch ID, or PIN; auto-locks on background
- Hide app preview in task switcher so it's invisible at a glance
- Private notifications (show only "Caelyn reminder" on lock screen, never details)

**No Server**
- Caelyn runs no servers and makes no network calls of its own
- No account, no email, no phone number, no signup required
- HealthKit export is one-way into Apple's health app (your choice)
- Optional iCloud Sync sends an encrypted copy to *your* private iCloud—never Caelyn's

**No Tracking**
- Zero third-party SDKs, zero analytics, zero ads, zero data selling
- Apple's Privacy Manifest declares no tracking; App Store confirms NSPrivacyTracking=false
- No internet permission in Info.plist
- What leaves your phone: what you export, what you sync to Health, what you back up to your own iCloud, and—only once—app purchase validation through Apple

**Survives Legal Compulsion**
- Because Caelyn holds no data and runs no servers, there is nothing to subpoena, sell, or breach
- Duress PIN silently and permanently wipes everything, then opens the app looking fresh and empty
- This isn't a policy claim; it's the architecture

#### What's Different

**Caelyn vs Every Other Tracker:**
- Competitors' AI lives on their servers (data lock-in, breach risk, subpoena risk)
- Caelyn's AI lives on your phone (same learning, zero risk)
- Every other app asks for your email or creates an account
- Caelyn never asks who you are
- All other trackers monetize your data or sell ads
- Caelyn doesn't collect data to sell; we make money from subscriptions, not surveillance

**For Doctors & Researchers:**
- PDF clinical report with summary, insights, timeline, and data—export anytime
- Compatible with Apple Health for cycle and symptom data
- No barriers to data portability; your data is yours
- CSV export works with research and import tools

## Age Rating Recommendation

**12+**

### Rating Reasoning

| Category | Assessment | Justification |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| **Frequent/Intense** | No mature content | Cycle tracking and health logging are appropriate topics for teens and adults; the app uses clinical language (menstrual flow, ovulation) without sensationalism. |
| **Infrequent/Mild** | Sexual Content (Mild) | App allows optional logging of sexual activity and pregnancy testing, but these are data fields—not content, media, or discussion. Neutral, clinical logging only. |
| **Infrequent/Mild** | Medical Content (Mild) | App is a health tracker with symptom logging and educational phase guides; content is factual and non-alarming. |
| **None** | Violence, Profanity, Alcohol/Tobacco, Gambling | Not applicable. |
| **No Restriction** | Unrestricted Web Access | App makes no outbound network calls; no web browser embedded; no open internet access. |

### Why 12+ Is Appropriate

- **Empowerment, not concealment:** Caelyn doesn't hide menstruation or cycle health; it normalizes them with clinical language and compassionate design. A 12-year-old tracking her first period should feel informed and supported, not ashamed.
- **No adult content:** Sexual activity logging is optional metadata, not narrative or media.
- **No medical diagnosis or prescription:** Educational phase guides explain what's normal, not what's wrong.
- **Privacy & trust:** The app teaches data control and digital literacy (PIN, encryption, no accounts)—skills every teen should learn.
- **Parental involvement welcomed:** Parents can help set up the app, review privacy settings, and discuss their child's cycle health—the app supports that conversation.

**Alternative: 4+** (if App Store interprets health tracking as general wellness with no mature elements; less conservative but defensible.)

**Do not use: 17+** (would discourage teen users who most benefit from the privacy & education; overstates the maturity level of clinical menstrual tracking.)

**Summary:** Caelyn does not collect, track, or sell health data. Cycle logs and insights are generated and stored on the user's device. If iCloud Sync is enabled (opt-in, off by default), a copy is encrypted and sent to the user's own Apple account—never Caelyn's servers. Purchase checks are handled directly by Apple through StoreKit 2. No third-party SDKs or analytics.

### Export Compliance

- **Encryption Used:** HTTPS (for purchase validation only), Apple's CloudKit encryption (if Sync enabled), Keychain for PIN hash
- **Exemption:** Standard exemption (encryption is Apple-provided, used only for authentication and user privacy, not for information protection of controlled data)
